My Projects

Check out some of the projects I have worked on.

US Honey Production, Demand, and how to Maximize Sales

Developed a Power BI dashboard on U.S. honey production (1995-2021) using USDA data. Key insights include rising prices, declining colony yields, and top producers like North Dakota. Introduced “Average Monetary Yield per Colony” to highlight Virginia’s potential. The project features three dashboards: trends, state insights, and analysis.

Night Patrol Robot

I developed a night patrolling robot with an ESP32 camera for live video streaming and IoT connectivity. It autonomously patrols using IR sensors to detect human activity, avoid obstacles, and monitor sound. Controlled by an Arduino UNO and programmed in embedded C, the robot enhances security by detecting intrusions and reporting them to a central control unit.
